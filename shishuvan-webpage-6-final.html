Why does the school not use textbooks in classrooms?

Using textbooks limits students' knowledge to the content of the book whereas using the library and research material extends knowledge, as the library in itself is an entire book of knowledge and research. Using current information and relating concepts to daily experiences actively engages students, in developing a deeper understanding and comprehension of the subject matter. Teaching without a textbook requires more Prep Time, especially in the first few years. Teachers amass and adapt curriculum from a wide variety of sources, including journals, lab books, Web sites, packaged curricula, and other teachers. The data collected is adapted to meet the standards of individual classes and school.




Why is the school uniform made of Khadi?

With deference to Mahatma Gandhi's wishes, we at Shishuvan include Khadi curriculum to enable students to understand and appreciate the production of Khadi cloth from which their uniform is made of. The aim of the Khadi curriculum is to inculcate a respect for skilled labour and develop a meditative focus and attention in our students.




Why do students address teachers and other school staff by their first name?

The school's mission is to work together without creating any hierarchy among its members. Thus all school members including students, teachers, non teaching staff, administrative staff and parents address one another using their first names.

 




Why erasers are not used in classrooms?


Any errors made by students when writing are crossed off and corrected by students instead of using an eraser. This is to convey the message that it's alright to make a mistake which is to be identified and corrected. The proof of mistake and correction act as a reference to prevent repetition of similar mistakes in future.



 


Why teachers do not use red pen for correction?

The colour red is associated with danger and leaves a negative impression on the student's mind. It contradicts the idea and belief that it is alright to make mistakes and learn from them. Therefore the teachers do not use a red pen for correction.




Why does the school not support student participation in competitions in the Primary department?

Students at the Primary level must enjoy participating in events devoid of the pressure of winning or losing. As primary students are not mature to deal with the pressure of a competition the school does not support students' participation in competitions at their level.




Why is student participation in all school events mandatory?

The school's vision is holistic development and empowerment of students from all strata of society to deal with any global scenario. Therefore student participation in all school events is mandatory.

Why do the teachers not use symbols like * and - as remarks?

The school emphasizes on explanatory and personalized remarks highlighting the strengths of a student instead of general and symbolic remarks. Symbols may be use in addition to the explanatory remark. E.g. instead of the remark "Good", the remark could be "Good Job on Printing, Keep up the Good Work * "

How do students celebrate their birthday at school?

To celebrate their birthday in school the students dress up in clothes of their choice. They can bring in any Indian sweet to share with their classmates.
